Why Measure in Millimeters?

Millimeters (mm) are the standard unit for engineering, manufacturing, and detailed design work. Whether you are checking a part diagram, a floor plan, or a sewing pattern, precision matters.

PDF Ruler defaults to metric measurements, making it the perfect tool for:

  • Detailed Schematics: Verify small component sizes.
  • Print Layouts: Ensure bleed and safety zones are correct (e.g., 3mm bleed).
  • Crafting: Check pattern pieces before printing to avoid wasting paper.

How to Use

Simply upload your PDF and drag a line. The tool will display the length in centimeters (cm). To convert to millimeters, simply multiply by 10 (e.g., 2.54 cm = 25.4 mm).

Common Questions

Is it accurate?

Yes, assuming the document is standard A4 size. We use the PDF's internal geometry to calculate distance.

Can I switch directly to mm?

Currently, we show cm, but a unit switcher (mm/in) is widely requested and coming in the next update!
