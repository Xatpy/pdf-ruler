Why Measure PDFs in the Browser?

Traditionally, measuring distance in a PDF required downloading heavy software like Adobe Acrobat Pro or Bluebeam. These tools are expensive and complex. PDF Ruler changes that by providing a lightweight, browser-based solution that runs entirely on your device.

  • Privacy First: Your documents never leave your computer.
  • Instant: No installation or signup required.
  • Accurate: Calibrated for standard A4 documents.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Open the Tool: Go to the PDF Ruler Homepage.
  2. Load Your PDF: Click "Open PDF" to select your file.
  3. Draw to Measure: Click and drag anywhere on the page to create a measurement line.
  4. Read Values: The distance in centimeters (cm) is displayed instantly.

Common Questions

Can I measure distance in a scanned PDF?

Yes. Since we use a visual overlay, you can measure distance on any visual element, even if it's a scanned image.

Is it accurate for blueprints?

For standard scale drawings on A4 paper, it is very accurate. For other scales, we are adding manual calibration soon.
