Accuracy & Calibration

Auto-Calibration (A4): This tool currently assumes your PDF page is standard A4 size (210mm x 297mm). Measurements will be accurate for A4 documents or documents scaled to A4.

Note: For architectural drawings with specific scales (e.g., 1:50, 1:100) or non-A4 paper sizes, please ensure your print settings match or use relative measurements. Manual calibration is coming soon.

Privacy & Security

Local-only No Uploads Open Source

Unlike other online PDF tools, PDF Ruler runs generic JavaScript entirely in your browser. We do not have a backend server to store your files. You can even disconnect from the internet after loading the page, and the tool will continue to work perfectly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF ruler free?

Yes, it is 100% free and open source.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once the page is loaded, you don't need an internet connection.

Can I measure feet or inches?

Currently, we display measurements in centimeters (cm). You can easily convert to other units.

Is my document uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens locally on your device using HTML5 canvas and PDF.js.
