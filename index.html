Privacy & Security

  • Works offline: Disconnect your internet and it still works.
  • Local processing: Your PDF files are processed entirely on your device.

  • No tracking: No analytics, no cookies, no pixels.

Verify it: Open Developer Tools → Network tab. You will see 0 requests when you load a PDF.
